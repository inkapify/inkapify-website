Questions

Does donating unlock Pro features? +
No — donations and Pro subscriptions are completely separate. Donating is a voluntary way to say thank you. If you want Pro features, upgrade via the app for $4.99/month or $39.99/year.
Can I donate more than once? +
Yes — absolutely. There is no limit. You can donate as many times as you like, any amount, any time. Each donation is a completely separate one-time payment.
How is the donation processed? +
Donations are processed securely through Google Play billing — the same trusted system used for all app purchases. Your payment details are never stored by Inkapify.
Will I get a receipt? +
Yes — Google Play sends an email receipt to your Gmail address after every purchase, just like any other Google Play transaction.
