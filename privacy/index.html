Privacy Policy
Effective Date: March 13, 2026
Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains exactly what data Inkapify collects, how it is used, and how it is protected. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers.

1 Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account details: name, email address, and password when you register
  • Financial data: income, expenses, transactions, budgets, goals, and debt records you enter
  • Categories and tags you create to organize your transactions
  • Recurring rules, reminders, and bill schedules you configure
  • Profile preferences and app settings

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • App usage data: features used, screens visited, session duration
  • Crash reports and error logs to help us improve the app
  • Push notification tokens (only to deliver reminders you request)

Inka AI Conversation Data

  • Questions you ask Inka AI and Finn's responses (session-only — deleted when you close the screen)
  • Aggregated financial summaries sent to Anthropic's API to generate Finn's responses (see Section 4A)
  • A count of Inka AI questions used per month (for usage limit enforcement)

What We Do NOT Collect

✓  We do not connect to your bank accounts or financial institutions
✓  We do not collect payment card numbers or banking credentials
✓  We do not access your contacts, camera, or microphone
✓  We do not collect your GPS location
✓  We do not send raw transactions, account numbers, or personal identifiers to AI services

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Inkapify app and its features
  • Sync your financial data securely across your devices
  • Send push notifications for reminders and alerts you have configured
  • Respond to your support requests and resolve technical issues
  • Improve app performance, stability, and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations
✓  We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
✓  We do not use your financial data for advertising purposes.

3 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud infrastructure provider. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL and encrypted at rest using AES-256.

Our security measures include:

  • Row-level security policies — you can only ever access your own data
  • Encrypted database connections for all communications
  • Secure authentication with hashed passwords (never stored in plain text)
  • Regular security monitoring and updates

While we implement strong security measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We are committed to protecting your data to the best of our ability.

4 Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We share limited data only with the following trusted service providers that are necessary to run the app:

Service Providers

  • Supabase — database hosting and authentication. Your data is stored on their servers under strict security controls.
  • Expo / EAS — app build and over-the-air update delivery infrastructure.
  • Anthropic — AI processing for the Inka AI assistant feature. See Section 4A below for full details on what data is shared with Anthropic.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management and in-app purchase processing. Only your anonymous user ID and subscription status are shared.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities such as a court order.

Business Transfers

If Inkapify is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred. We will provide notice before your data is transferred and subject to a different privacy policy.

4A Inka AI — How Your Data Is Used

Inkapify includes an AI-powered assistant called Finn, powered by Anthropic's Claude API. This section explains exactly what data is shared with Anthropic when you use Inka AI.

What We Send to Anthropic

When you ask Finn a question, we send only aggregated, anonymized financial summaries — never your raw data or personal identifiers. Specifically, we send:

✓  Total income and expenses for the current month (single numbers only)
✓  Top spending categories by name and total amount
✓  Budget names, percentage used, and remaining amount
✓  Count and total value of upcoming bills (no bill names)
✓  Investment portfolio total value only (no institution names)
✓  Total loan and fixed deposit values (no account numbers)
✓  Goal and debt summary counts (no goal or person names)
✓  Your question and the last 8 messages of the conversation

What We Never Send to Anthropic

✗  Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
✗  Raw transaction records, notes, or receipt images
✗  Account names, numbers, or institution names
✗  Loan lender names or account numbers
✗  Bank names, FD account numbers
✗  Payee names, debt person names, or nominees
✗  Goal names, investment names, or any descriptive labels
✗  Any internal IDs or unique identifiers

Conversation Storage

Inka AI conversations are session-only. Your conversation is temporarily stored in our database while the Finn screen is open so that an interrupted session can be resumed. When you close the Finn screen, your conversation is permanently deleted from our servers. Conversations do not persist between sessions.

Usage Limits

To manage service costs and ensure fair access, Inka AI questions are subject to monthly usage limits based on your subscription tier. Your usage count is tracked server-side in our database.

Anthropic's Data Practices

Anthropic may process the aggregated summaries we send in accordance with their privacy policy. We have configured our API usage to opt out of Anthropic using our data for model training where this option is available. For full details on how Anthropic handles data, see anthropic.com/privacy.

Opting Out of Inka AI

Inka AI is an optional feature. If you prefer not to use it, simply do not open the Inka AI screen. Your financial data is never sent to Anthropic unless you actively ask Finn a question.

5 Data Retention

  • Your financial records are retained while your account is active
  • Deleted records are removed from your visible data immediately
  • Inka AI conversations are deleted immediately when you close the Finn screen
  • Inka AI usage counts (question log) are retained for up to 13 months for monthly limit calculations, then deleted
  • When you delete your account, all personal data including AI usage logs is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days
  • Anonymized, aggregated usage statistics may be retained as they cannot be linked back to you

  • Correction — edit or update any information you have entered
  • Deletion — delete individual records, or delete your entire account and all associated data
  • Export — export your transaction data from within the app
  • Notifications — enable or disable push notifications in your device settings or within the app
