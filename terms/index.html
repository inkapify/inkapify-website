2 Description of the App

Inkapify is a personal finance tracking application that allows users to manually record and organise their income, expenses, transfers, budgets, goals, debts, recurring transactions, and investments. The app includes an optional AI-powered assistant called Inka, which answers questions about your personal financial data using aggregated summaries.

⚠️  Inkapify is a manual tracking tool only. The App does not connect to bank accounts, financial institutions, or payment systems. All data is entered by you manually. Inkapify does not provide financial advice, investment advice, tax advice, or any professional financial services.

Any financial insights or summaries generated by the App, including those generated by the Inka AI assistant, are for informational and organisational purposes only. Inka AI responses are generated by an AI model and may not always be accurate. Always consult a qualified financial professional for financial decisions.

Offline Functionality

Inkapify is designed to work fully offline. Core features — including adding and viewing transactions, managing budgets, tracking goals and debts, and accessing reports — function without an internet connection. Your data is saved to your device first and synced to cloud backup automatically when online.

ℹ️  Receipt photo uploads require an active internet connection. While transactions save locally when offline, receipt images can only be uploaded once the device is back online. The transaction itself (without the receipt) is always saved immediately.

4B PDF Import Terms of Use

The PDF bank statement import feature (Pro plan only) uses Google's Gemini AI to classify extracted transaction data. By using PDF Import, you agree to the following:

How Data Is Processed

  • Your PDF is processed on our secure servers — the file itself is never forwarded to any AI service
  • Only extracted transaction text (merchant names, dates, amounts) is sent to Google's Gemini AI for classification of unmatched rows
  • The PDF is not stored by Inkapify after processing completes

Accuracy

⚠️  AI-generated transaction classifications may be inaccurate. Always review the import preview screen before confirming. You are responsible for verifying all imported data.

Privacy

Only extracted transaction text is sent to Google's Gemini AI — never the raw PDF, account numbers, banking credentials, or personal identifiers. We use the Gemini API on a paid plan, under which Google does not use your data to train their AI models. See our Privacy Policy Section 4B for full details.

Google's Terms

Your use of PDF Import is also subject to Google's Gemini API usage policies. See Google Gemini API Terms.
