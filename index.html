FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before you download.

Does Inkapify connect to my bank? +
No. Inkapify never connects to your bank accounts or requests banking credentials. All transactions are entered manually or imported via CSV. This is intentional — your banking passwords never touch our servers.
Is my financial data safe? +
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS/SSL). Row-level security means your data is completely isolated. You can enable App Lock with fingerprint or PIN. Delete your account and all data at any time from within the app.
How does Inka AI work? +
Inka answers questions using aggregated summaries of your data — not raw transactions. We send only totals and percentages to the AI model. No account numbers, transaction notes, names, or identifiers are shared. Conversations are deleted when you close the chat.
What's included in the free plan? +
The free plan includes unlimited transactions, up to 3 accounts, 3 budgets, 5 recurring rules, 15 Inka AI questions per month, and 90 days of history. Pro unlocks unlimited everything, full history, all 8 reports, CSV import/export, and 200 Inka AI questions per month.
Can I import my existing transactions? +
Yes. Pro users can import transactions from any bank via CSV. The importer automatically detects transfer pairs and handles multiple currencies. Free users can export their data at any time.
Is iOS available? +
Not yet — Inkapify is Android-first. iOS is planned and will be announced when available. Email [email protected] to be notified when iOS launches.
